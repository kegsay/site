Things to be aware of

If you book in any of the hotels, please let us know where you are staying in the google form, as there will be a free taxi service at 11:30am to take you to the wedding venue. The taxi will then do drop offs at your hotels at 21:30pm, 00:00 and 2:15am. All times are subject to change but not by too much!

The wedding will take place outside (weather permitting) on grass. Paths are cobbled and the reception will be in the Tipi’s which have slatted wooden flooring. Please dress accordingly as the nearest hospital is 49 miles away (1 hour and 15 minutes away) 😉😂

We would love it if you would join us for a meal on Monday 4th August at 7pm. Restaurant to be confirmed in due course.

If you’d like to make a mini holiday of it, we’ll be doing some activities throughout the week, including Archery, Axe Throwing and Alpaca walking. Please let us know which, if any, you'd like to attend on the google form.
